## 4. Phone Call Script

### Opening (First 30 Seconds)
```
YOU: "Namaste, may I speak with [Dealer Name]?"

THEM: "Speaking" / "This is [name]"

YOU: "Hi [Name], this is [Your Name] calling from MyRideNepal. Do you have 2-3 minutes? I promise to be quick."

THEM: "What's this about?"

YOU: "We're launching a free online marketplace specifically for bikes and scooters in Nepal, and we're inviting dealers like [Dealer Business Name] to join our beta program. It's completely free and takes about 15 minutes to set up. Would you like to hear more?"
```

### Pitch (If Interested - 1-2 Minutes)
```
YOU: "Great! So MyRideNepal is like Facebook Marketplace, but only for bikes and scooters. Here's what makes it useful for dealers:

1. You get unlimited free listings for all your bikes
2. You get a verified dealer profile page with your own URL
3. Buyers can contact you directly via WhatsApp or phone
4. We don't charge any commission — ever
5. You can see analytics on which bikes get the most views

We're starting with 30-50 dealers during our 3-month free beta to make sure everything works well for the Nepal market.

How many bikes do you usually have in your showroom?"

THEM: [Answer]

YOU: "Perfect. You could list all of them for free. Each listing gets its own page with photos, price, specs — and buyers searching for [their brands] would find them easily.

Would you like me to help you get started? It takes about 15 minutes and I can walk you through it on the phone or come to your showroom."
```

### Closing (30 Seconds)
```
IF INTERESTED:
YOU: "Excellent! When works better for you — today afternoon or tomorrow morning?"

IF NOT SURE:
YOU: "I understand. How about I send you the details on WhatsApp and you can look at it when you have time? What's your WhatsApp number?"

IF NOT INTERESTED:
YOU: "No problem at all. Can I follow up with you in a few weeks after we've launched? Maybe you'll have some feedback after seeing it."
```

**Call Duration:** 3-5 minutes ideal, max 10 minutes  
**Best Time:** 10:30am-12pm or 2pm-4pm (avoid lunch 12-2pm)

## 8. Objection Handling

### Objection 1: "Is it free?"

**Response Option 1:**
```
"Yes, completely free! We don't charge dealers or buyers. No listing fees, no commission on sales. The beta is free for 3 months, and we're planning to keep basic features free even after that."
```

**Response Option 2:**
```
"Great question! Yes, it's 100% free right now. We're not charging anything during the beta. Our goal is to build a great platform first, then figure out optional premium features later — but basic listings will always be free."
```

**Follow-up:**  
"No credit card required, no hidden fees. If we ever charge for anything, we'll tell you way in advance."

---

### Objection 2: "What happens after 3 months?"

**Response Option 1:**
```
"Good question! After 3 months, we'll review with all beta dealers. We're planning to keep basic features free forever — but might introduce optional premium features like promoted listings or advanced analytics. Beta dealers will get special pricing on anything premium."
```

**Response Option 2:**
```
"Honestly, we're figuring that out! Right now our focus is making the platform useful. Basic features (listings, profile, buyer contact) will stay free. If we add paid features, beta dealers will always get the best deal — and we'll ask for your input first."
```

**Follow-up:**  
"Worst case — you try it free for 3 months, then decide if you want to continue. But we're planning to keep it free."

---

### Objection 3: "Do I need to pay commission?"

**Response Option 1:**
```
"No commission at all! That's the whole point. Buyers contact you directly on WhatsApp or phone, you talk to them, show the bike, negotiate — then you complete the sale directly. We don't get involved in the transaction, so there's no commission."
```

**Response Option 2:**
```
"Zero commission. We're not a middleman. We just connect buyers to your listings. When someone's interested, they call or WhatsApp you directly. It's like Facebook Marketplace — we just help them find you."
```

**Follow-up:**  
"No platform fees, no transaction fees, nothing. You keep 100% of the sale price."

---

### Objection 4: "I already use Facebook, why do I need this?"

**Response Option 1:**
```
"That's smart — Facebook is great! But think of MyRideNepal as a complement, not a replacement. Here's the difference:

Facebook: Posts get lost after a day, buyers see furniture and phones mixed with bikes, no permanent showroom page.

MyRideNepal: Your listings stay up permanently, buyers searching for bikes ONLY find you, you get your own verified dealer page, and you can see which bikes get the most views.

Many dealers use both — Facebook for quick posts, MyRideNepal for a permanent online showroom. And hey, it's free, so why not try both?"
```

**Response Option 2:**
```
"Fair point! Facebook is where everyone is. But here's what dealers tell us:

1. Facebook posts disappear quickly
2. Hard to track which bikes people are interested in  
3. Buyers ask the same questions over and over
4. No way to showcase your full inventory in one place

MyRideNepal gives you a permanent showroom link you can share: myridenepal.com/dealers/[your-name]. Plus, we're building tools specifically for dealers like analytics and share buttons.

Worth trying both for 3 months and seeing if it helps?"
```

## Follow-Up Sequence

### Day 0: Initial Contact (WhatsApp/Call)
- Send initial message
- Wait for response
- If responds within 2 hours: continue conversation
- If no response: wait 3 days

### Day 3: First Follow-Up
- Gentle reminder
- Add value (new info or social proof)
- Ask if timing is better now

### Day 7: Second Follow-Up
- Different approach (question-based)
- Acknowledge they might be busy
- Offer to send details for later

### Day 14: Final Follow-Up
- Last touchpoint
- Create soft urgency (beta filling up)
- Leave door open for future contact

### After Day 14:
- Mark as "Not Interested" or "Follow up later"
- Don't spam — respect their decision
- Can retry in 30-60 days with different message

## Do's and Don'ts

### DO:
✅ Use their name  
✅ Mention their showroom name  
✅ Be brief (respect their time)  
✅ Emphasize "free" and "no commission"  
✅ Offer to help with setup  
✅ Use WhatsApp (most dealers prefer it)  
✅ Be persistent but polite (3 follow-ups max)  
✅ Build trust (share website, dealer examples)

### DON'T:
❌ Send generic copy-paste messages  
❌ Call during peak hours (morning rush, closing time)  
❌ Spam them daily  
❌ Over-promise ("You'll get 100 buyers!")  
❌ Be pushy or aggressive  
❌ Use overly technical language  
❌ Hide that it's a beta/new platform  
❌ Claim fake numbers ("Join 1000 dealers" if you only have 10)
