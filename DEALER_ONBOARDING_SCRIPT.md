# Dealer Onboarding Script

**Purpose:** Step-by-step script for onboarding dealers onto MyRideNepal  
**Date:** 22 May 2026  
**Duration:** 15-30 minutes per dealer  
**Mode:** Phone call, video call, or in-person

---

## Pre-Call Preparation

**Before calling dealer:**
- [ ] Review dealer's business (visit their Facebook page/location)
- [ ] Note their brands (Hero, Honda, Pulsar, etc.)
- [ ] Check their approximate inventory size (from photos)
- [ ] Have their contact info ready (WhatsApp, phone)
- [ ] Open myridenepal.com/dealer-signup on your screen
- [ ] Have DEALER_OUTREACH_TRACKER open to update notes

**Materials Needed:**
- Computer/tablet to show platform (if video/in-person)
- Dealer signup link ready to send
- Example dealer profile to show (reference)
- Note-taking app for dealer details

---

## Call Structure

**Total Time:** 15-30 minutes

1. **Introduction & Rapport** (2-3 min)
2. **Information Collection** (5-7 min)
3. **Platform Walkthrough** (5-8 min)
4. **Profile Creation** (3-5 min)
5. **First Listing Help** (Optional: 5-10 min)
6. **Next Steps & Close** (2-3 min)

---

## PART 1: Introduction & Rapport (2-3 minutes)

### Opening

```
YOU: "Namaste [Dealer Name], this is [Your Name] from MyRideNepal. Thanks for taking my call! Did I catch you at a good time?"

THEM: [Response]

YOU: "Great! This should take about 15-20 minutes. I'm going to help you set up your free dealer profile so you can start listing your bikes online. Sound good?"

THEM: [Response]

YOU: "Perfect. Before we start, can I ask — have you looked at the myridenepal.com website at all, or is this your first time hearing about it?"
```

**If they've seen the website:**
```
YOU: "Awesome! What did you think? Any questions before we get started?"
```

**If they haven't seen it:**
```
YOU: "No problem! Let me give you a quick 30-second overview so you know what we're setting up..."

[Give elevator pitch from LAUNCH_MESSAGING_KIT.md]

"Make sense?"
```

### Build Rapport (Optional 1-2 min)

```

## PART 2: Information Collection (5-7 minutes)

### Required Information Checklist

**Explain First:**
```
YOU: "Okay, let's get your profile set up. I'll need some basic information about your showroom. Most of this you probably already have — it's stuff you'd put on a business card. Ready?"
```

### 1. Business Information

```
YOU: "First, what's the official name of your showroom or business?"

THEM: [Business name]

YOU: "Great. And how do you want it to appear on the website? Same as [business name], or any different spelling?"

THEM: [Confirmation]

YOU: "Perfect. Now, where is your showroom located? Which district and specific area?"

THEM: [Location]

YOU: "Got it. And what's the full address I should put? Just the road name and area is fine."

THEM: [Address]
```

**Write down:**
- ✅ Business name (exact spelling)
- ✅ District
- ✅ City/Area
- ✅ Full address

---

### 2. Contact Information

```
YOU: "Now for contact details. What's the best phone number for buyers to reach you?"

THEM: [Phone number]

YOU: "Is that the same as your WhatsApp number, or do you have a separate WhatsApp for business?"

THEM: [WhatsApp number]

YOU: "Perfect. And do you have an email for the business? If not, no problem — we can skip that."

THEM: [Email or "No email"]
```

**Write down:**
- ✅ Phone number (format: 98XXXXXXXX)
- ✅ WhatsApp number (same or different)
- ✅ Email (optional)

---

### 3. Business Description

```
YOU: "This next part helps buyers know more about your showroom. Can you describe your business in 2-3 sentences? Like, what makes [Dealer Business Name] special? What brands you focus on, how long you've been in business, things like that."

THEM: [Description]

YOU: "Great! I'll write that down. Feel free to tell me more — I can make it sound professional if needed."

THEM: [More details]

YOU: "Perfect, I've got: '[Read back polished version]'. Does that sound good?"
```

**Write down:**
- ✅ Business description (100-200 words)
- Polish if needed:
  - Mention years in business
  - Brands carried
  - Service quality
  - Location convenience
  - Any specialties

**Example template:**
```
"[Business Name] is a trusted [brand] dealer in [location] with [X] years of experience. We specialize in [brands] and offer both new and used bikes with complete documentation. Our showroom is conveniently located in [area] with professional service and competitive pricing."
```

---

```
YOU: "What are your showroom hours? When can buyers visit?"

THEM: [Hours]

YOU: "And is that 7 days a week, or do you close on certain days?"

THEM: [Days]

YOU: "Got it. So I'll put: Open [Days] from [Time] to [Time]. Sound good?"
```

**Write down:**
- ✅ Opening time
- ✅ Closing time
- ✅ Days open (e.g., "Sunday-Friday" or "Daily")

```
YOU: "What brands do you sell? Just list the main ones — Hero, Honda, etc."

THEM: [List of brands]

YOU: "Got it."
```

**Write down:**
- ✅ Brands (Hero, Honda, Pulsar, Yamaha, KTM, TVS, Suzuki, Royal Enfield, etc.)
- ✅ New/Used/Both

### 6. Images (Logo & Banner)

```
YOU: "Last thing — do you have a logo for your business? If yes, can you send it to me on WhatsApp?"

THEM: [Yes/No]

IF YES:

## PART 3: Platform Walkthrough (5-8 minutes)

### Show Dealer Profile Example

```
YOU: "Perfect! I've got all your info. Now let me show you what your dealer profile will look like. Are you near a computer or phone where you can look at a website right now?"

THEM: [Yes/No]

IF YES:
YOU: "Great! Go to myridenepal.com/dealers and pick any dealer to click on. That's what yours will look like."

[Wait for them to navigate]

YOU: "See how it shows the business name, location, contact buttons, and all the bike listings? That's what buyers will see when they find you."

IF NO:
YOU: "No problem, I can send you screenshots later. But basically, you get a full page with your business info, contact buttons, and all your bike listings in one place."
```

---

### Explain Key Features

```
YOU: "Let me quickly walk you through what you get as a dealer:

1. Your Own Page: You get a dedicated URL like myridenepal.com/dealers/[your-business-name]. You can share this link anywhere — Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp.

2. Verified Badge: Once we review your profile, you'll get a verified checkmark. This builds trust with buyers.

3. Unlimited Listings: You can add as many bikes as you want — 5, 10, 50, doesn't matter. All free.

4. Direct Contact: When buyers are interested, they click 'Contact Dealer' and it opens WhatsApp or phone with your number. No forms, no waiting — direct contact.

5. Analytics: You can see how many people viewed your profile, which bikes got the most clicks, where buyers are coming from.

6. Share Tools: You can generate social media captions for Facebook, Instagram, TikTok — makes it easy to promote your bikes.

Make sense so far?"
```

**Pause for questions**

---

### Show Dashboard (If Possible)

```
YOU: "Once you're set up, you'll have a dashboard where you manage everything. You can:
- Add new listings
- Edit existing bikes
- See your analytics
- Generate share captions
- Update your profile

It's all from your phone — super simple."

[If showing screen, navigate to dealer dashboard]

YOU: "See how clean it is? Most dealers tell us it's easier than Facebook."
```

## PART 4: Profile Creation (3-5 minutes)

### Account Creation

```
YOU: "Okay, let's actually create your account now. Do you have an email you use regularly?"

THEM: [Email]

YOU: "Perfect. I'm going to send you a link on WhatsApp. When you open it, you'll see a signup form. Can you click on it now?"

[Send link: myridenepal.com/dealer-signup]

YOU: "Got it? Okay, go ahead and enter your email and create a password. Anything you'll remember — doesn't need to be super complex."

[Wait]

YOU: "Done? Great! You'll get a verification email. Can you check your inbox real quick and click the link?"

[Wait]

YOU: "Perfect! Now you're logged in. You should see the dealer signup form."
```

---

### Fill Out Profile Form Together

```
YOU: "Okay, I'm going to walk you through this form. It's the same info we just collected, so it'll be quick."

FIELD BY FIELD:

"Business Name: Enter [Business Name]"
"District: Select [District] from dropdown"
"City: Type [City/Area]"
"Address: Put [Full Address]"
"Phone: [Phone Number]"
"WhatsApp: [WhatsApp Number]"
"Email: [Email]" (optional)
"Description: I'll help you with this — copy what I say..."
[Read description you polished earlier]

"Opening Hours: Select [Opening Time] to [Closing Time]"
"Days Open: Check the boxes for [Days]"

"Brands Carried: Check [Hero], [Honda], [Yamaha], etc."

"Do you sell new or used?: Select [New/Used/Both]"

YOU: "Okay, that's everything! Hit 'Submit' at the bottom."

[Wait]

YOU: "Awesome! You're all set. You should see a confirmation message."
```

---

### Add Logo/Banner (If Available)

## PART 5: First Listing Help (Optional: 5-10 minutes)

### Offer to Help with First Listing

```
YOU: "Okay, your profile is done! Next step is adding your first bike. Do you have 5 more minutes right now, or would you rather do this later?"

IF YES:
YOU: "Great! Let's add one bike together so you know how it works. Pick any bike from your showroom."

THEM: [Describes bike]

YOU: "Perfect! Click 'Add Listing' in your dashboard."
```

---

### Walk Through Listing Form

```
YOU: "Okay, let's fill this out together:

Title: What's the full name? Like 'Hero Splendor Plus 2023'"
THEM: [Title]

Brand: Select [Brand] from dropdown

Model: Type [Model name]

Year: What year is it?
THEM: [Year]

Price: What are you asking for this bike? In NPR
THEM: [Price]

Condition: New, Excellent, Good, or Fair?
THEM: [Condition]

Mileage: How many kilometers? If it's new, put 0
THEM: [Mileage]

District: Should auto-fill with [District]

Description: Tell buyers about this bike. Mention:
- Any special features
- Why it's a good deal
- Service history if used
- Any accessories included

Want me to help you write that?"

[Help write 50-100 word description]

YOU: "Perfect! Now upload 3-5 photos of this bike. Do you have photos on your phone?"

IF YES:
YOU: "Great! Click 'Upload Photos' and select the best ones. Front, side, engine, bluebook — those are most important."

IF NO:
YOU: "No problem! You can add photos later. Just click 'Save as Draft' for now and upload photos when you have them."

YOU: "Okay, hit 'Submit Listing' when you're ready."

[Wait]

YOU: "Awesome! Your first bike is now live (or pending review if it's not auto-approved). See how easy that was?"
```

---

Think you can handle that?"

## Common Issues & Solutions

### Issue 1: Dealer doesn't have email
**Solution:**
```
"No problem! We can create one for you. How about [businessname]@gmail.com? You can set it up in 2 minutes on Gmail, and then we'll use that."
```

### Issue 2: Dealer doesn't have logo/photos
**Solution:**
```
"Totally fine! We can launch without them and add later. Your business name will show as text for now, and that's perfectly okay."
```

### Issue 3: Dealer not near computer during call
**Solution:**
```
"No worries! I can create your profile with the info you gave me, then send you the login details. You can add your first listing later when you're free. Sound good?"
```

### Issue 4: Dealer is confused by form
**Solution:**
```
"I know it seems like a lot of fields, but think of it like filling out a Facebook Business Page. Same information, just organized differently. I'll walk you through each one — it's easier than it looks!"
```

### Issue 5: Dealer wants to add many bikes now
**Solution:**
```
"Love the enthusiasm! But let's just do 1-2 bikes together right now so you learn the process. Then you can add the rest at your own pace. It's actually faster when you do it yourself because you know your inventory best!"
```
