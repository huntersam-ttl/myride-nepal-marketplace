# Seed Data Plan for Soft Launch

**Purpose:** Curated, high-quality seed data for MyRideNepal soft launch  
**Date:** 22 May 2026  
**Target:** 20-30 listings, 3-5 dealers, clean presentation  
**Timeline:** 2-3 hours of curation work

---

## Overview

**Goal:** Launch with enough quality content to demonstrate value without looking empty, but keep expectations realistic for a new marketplace.

**Not aiming for:**
- Thousands of listings (unrealistic for new platform)
- Dozens of dealers (soft launch phase)
- Fake volume numbers

**Aiming for:**
- Quality over quantity
- Real or realistic-looking listings
- Functional dealer profiles
- Clean, trustworthy presentation

## Listing Quality Standards

### ✅ Required for Each Listing

**Basic Info:**
- ✅ Proper title: "[Brand] [Model] [Year]" (e.g., "Pulsar NS 200 2020")
- ✅ Accurate brand from POPULAR_BRANDS list
- ✅ Model name (real model, not "Test Model")
- ✅ Year: 2015-2024 (realistic range)
- ✅ Price: NPR 100,000 - NPR 800,000 (realistic Nepal prices)
- ✅ Mileage: 1,000 - 80,000 km (realistic usage)
- ✅ District: Real Nepal district from NEPAL_DISTRICTS
- ✅ Condition: "New", "Excellent", "Good", "Fair" (not "Test")

**Images:**
- ✅ Minimum 3 clear photos
- ✅ Different angles: front, side, odometer/details
- ✅ Good lighting, not blurry
- ✅ Real bike photos (not stock photos if possible)
- ✅ No watermarks or "SOLD" overlays

**Description:**
- ✅ 50-200 words
- ✅ Mentions features, condition, reason for selling
- ✅ No "test", "dummy", "fake" keywords
- ✅ Natural language (not template-y)
- ✅ Can mention negotiable price if realistic

**Contact:**
- ✅ Valid Nepal mobile: 98XXXXXXXX format
- ✅ WhatsApp enabled (same or different number)
- ✅ Realistic seller name

**Documents (Optional but Good):**
- ✅ Bluebook: Yes/No
- ✅ Insurance: Yes/No
- ✅ Tax clearance: Yes/No
- ✅ Registration: Yes/No

### 🚫 Avoid

- ❌ Titles with "Test", "Sample", "Delete"
- ❌ Unrealistic prices: NPR 1, NPR 9999999
- ❌ Placeholder descriptions: "Lorem ipsum", "TBD"
- ❌ Fake phone numbers: 1234567890, 9800000000
- ❌ Non-Nepal districts: "Test District"
- ❌ Stock photos with dealer logos
- ❌ Blurry or inappropriate images

## Dealer Quality Standards

### ✅ Required for Each Dealer

**Basic Profile:**
- ✅ Business name: Real-sounding name (e.g., "Kathmandu Motors", "Valley Bikes")
- ✅ District: Real Nepal district
- ✅ City: Real city name
- ✅ Phone: Valid Nepal mobile (98XXXXXXXX)
- ✅ WhatsApp: Same or different valid number
- ✅ Email: Professional email (optional)

**Branding:**
- ✅ Logo: Clean, professional logo image
- ✅ Banner: Shop/showroom photo or branded banner
- ✅ Showroom photos: 2-5 photos of actual showroom (optional)

**Details:**
- ✅ Brands carried: 2-5 realistic brands (Hero, Honda, Yamaha, etc.)
- ✅ Opening hours: Realistic business hours (e.g., "9 AM - 7 PM")
- ✅ Description: 100-200 words about dealership
- ✅ Services: "Sales, Service, Parts" or similar

**Inventory:**
- ✅ Minimum 5 active listings
- ✅ Maximum 15 listings (keep it realistic)
- ✅ All listings meet quality standards above
- ✅ Mix of brands dealer carries

**Verification:**
- ✅ Verified badge: true (for seed dealers)
- ✅ Onboarding complete
- ✅ Beta participant if during beta period

### 🚫 Avoid

- ❌ Names: "Test Dealer", "Demo Shop", "Delete Me"
- ❌ Fake contacts: 1234567890
- ❌ No logo or banner
- ❌ Empty inventory
- ❌ Single-brand only (unless intentional)
- ❌ Too many listings (>20 looks fake)

## Image Sourcing Strategy

### For Listings

**Option 1: Real Bikes (Preferred)**
- Ask friends/family with bikes to contribute
- Take photos of bikes in parking areas (with permission)
- Use marketplace photos from willing sellers
- Ensure no personal info visible

**Option 2: Stock Photos (If Needed)**
- Use Unsplash/Pexels bike photos
- Filter by "motorcycle nepal" or "bike nepal"
- Ensure high quality, no watermarks
- Mix with real photos if possible

**Option 3: Placeholder (Temporary)**
- Use branded bike images from manufacturers
- Clearly label as "Stock photo" if required
- Replace with real photos ASAP

### For Dealers

**Logo:**
- Simple text-based logo if no graphic
- Use brand colors (e.g., red/white for Hero dealer)
- Keep professional

**Banner:**
- Showroom exterior photo
- Interior showroom photo
- Or simple branded graphic

**Showroom Photos:**
- 2-5 photos of bikes on display
- Clean, well-lit showroom
- Professional appearance
