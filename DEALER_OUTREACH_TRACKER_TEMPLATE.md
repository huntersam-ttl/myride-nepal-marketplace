## Status Definitions

| Status | Definition | Next Action |
|--------|------------|-------------|
| **Not Contacted** | Haven't reached out yet | Make initial contact call/visit |
| **Contacted** | Initial call/message sent, awaiting response | Follow up if no response in 2-3 days |
| **Interested** | Expressed interest, needs more info | Send details, schedule demo/meeting |
| **Demo Scheduled** | Meeting/demo scheduled | Prepare materials, conduct demo |
| **Negotiating** | Discussing terms/features | Address concerns, clarify benefits |
| **Signup Pending** | Agreed to join, needs to sign up | Send signup link, assist with registration |
| **Onboarding** | Signed up, completing profile | Help with profile setup, listings upload |
| **Onboarded** | Active dealer with complete profile | Monitor activity, provide ongoing support |
| **Not Interested** | Declined to participate | Document reason, mark for future follow-up |
| **Unresponsive** | No response after multiple attempts | Try different contact method or wait |
| **On Hold** | Interested but timing not right | Schedule follow-up for later date |

## Priority Tiers

### Tier 1: High Priority (Target First)
- Large dealers with 20+ bikes in inventory
- Multi-brand dealers (Hero + Honda + Yamaha)
- Kathmandu Valley locations (high traffic)
- Established dealers with good reputation
- Dealers with online presence already

### Tier 2: Medium Priority
- Mid-size dealers with 10-20 bikes
- Single or dual-brand dealers
- Major city locations (Pokhara, Biratnagar, Bharatpur)
- Newer dealers looking to grow

### Tier 3: Lower Priority
- Small dealers with <10 bikes
- Remote district locations
- Limited brand selection
- Part-time sellers

## Outreach Methods

### 1. Phone Call (Preferred)
- **When:** First contact for warm leads
- **Duration:** 5-10 minutes
- **Script:** "Hello, I'm calling from MyRideNepal, Nepal's new bike marketplace. We're offering free dealer accounts during our beta program..."
- **Goal:** Gauge interest, schedule demo if interested

### 2. WhatsApp Message
- **When:** Initial contact or follow-up
- **Message:** "Namaste! We're MyRideNepal, launching a free bike marketplace for Nepal. We're inviting dealers like [Dealer Name] to join our beta program. Interested? [Link to dealer-beta page]"
- **Goal:** Get response, move to phone call

### 3. In-Person Visit
- **When:** For high-priority dealers or local dealers
- **Bring:** Laptop for demo, printed flyer, business card
- **Goal:** Build relationship, close onboarding on the spot

### 4. Email
- **When:** Follow-up or for dealers with professional email
- **Subject:** "Free Dealer Beta Invitation — MyRideNepal"
- **Goal:** Provide detailed information, schedule call

## Outreach Script Template

### Phone Script

**Introduction:**
"Namaste! My name is [Your Name] and I'm calling from MyRideNepal. Is this [Dealer Name]? ... Great! Do you have a few minutes to chat about a new opportunity for bike dealers in Nepal?"

**Pitch:**
"We've just launched MyRideNepal, a dedicated online marketplace for bikes and scooters across Nepal. We're offering free dealer accounts during our 3-month beta program. This includes:
- Unlimited free listings
- Your own dealer profile page
- Analytics dashboard to track views and leads
- Direct contact with buyers (no commission)
- Share tools for social media

We're looking for quality dealers like [Dealer Name] to be our launch partners."

**Handle Objections:**
- "How much does it cost?" → "It's completely free during the beta period, no hidden fees."
- "We already list on [other platform]" → "That's great! This is free and you can list on multiple platforms."
- "We're too busy" → "Setup takes only 15 minutes, and you can manage it anytime."
- "Is this real?" → "Yes, we're a Nepal-based startup. I can show you the site right now or visit in person."

**Close:**
"Would you be interested in creating a free account? I can help you set it up right now over the phone, or I can visit your shop this week. What works better for you?"

**Follow-up:**
"Perfect! I'll send you a WhatsApp message with the signup link and instructions. Can I follow up with you on [date] to see if you need any help?"

## Dealer Requirements

**Minimum to join beta:**
- ✅ Registered business in Nepal
- ✅ Physical showroom/location
- ✅ Valid phone/WhatsApp number
- ✅ At least 5 bikes to list initially
- ✅ Professional attitude and service

**Nice to have:**
- Logo image
- Showroom photos
- Multiple brands carried
- 10+ listings
- Social media presence

## Red Flags (Don't Onboard)

- ❌ Fake business or scam operation
- ❌ Poor reputation (many complaints)
- ❌ Unwilling to provide real contact info
- ❌ Demands payment or special treatment
- ❌ Aggressive or unprofessional behavior
- ❌ No actual inventory

- **Geographic spread:** At least 5 different districts represented
- **Quality:** 80%+ of dealers have complete profiles and 5+ listings
- **Activity:** 70%+ of dealers actively managing listings
- **Satisfaction:** Positive feedback from dealers on platform
