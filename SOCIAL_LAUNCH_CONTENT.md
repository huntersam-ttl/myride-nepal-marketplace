## 10. Safety-Focused Post

### Facebook Version
```
🛡️ Buying a Bike in Nepal? Stay Safe!

MyRideNepal is committed to making bike transactions safer. Here's our safety guide:

BEFORE VIEWING:
✅ Verify seller's identity (ask for ID)
✅ Check listing details carefully
✅ Look for verified badge
✅ Ask for bluebook photos upfront

DURING VIEWING:
✅ Meet in public place (dealer showroom or busy area)
✅ Bring a mechanic or experienced friend
✅ Check engine number matches bluebook
✅ Inspect for accident damage
✅ Test ride if possible
✅ Check all documents (insurance, tax clearance, registration)

RED FLAGS:
❌ Price too good to be true
❌ Seller refuses to show bluebook
❌ Insists on immediate payment
❌ Wants to meet in isolated area
❌ Bluebook has issues
❌ Engine number tampered with

PAYMENT SAFETY:
✅ Never pay full amount before ownership transfer
✅ Use bank transfer or eSewa (traceable)
✅ Get written receipt
✅ Complete ownership transfer at transport office
✅ Verify bluebook transfer completed

MYRIDENEPAL HELPS:
• Verified listings (we review before publishing)
• Seller contact info visible
• Safety tips on every page
• Report system for suspicious listings

Read full safety guide → myridenepal.com/safety-tips

Stay safe, ride safe! 🏍️🇳🇵

#BikeSafety #MyRideNepal #SafetyTips #NepalBikes #BuyerGuide #BikeBuyingTips
```
