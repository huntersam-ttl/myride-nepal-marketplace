## Key Message Principles

**DO:**
- ✅ Emphasize "free" and "no commission"
- ✅ Mention direct contact (WhatsApp/phone)
- ✅ Highlight Nepal-specific features
- ✅ Be honest about launch stage
- ✅ Use simple, clear language
- ✅ Focus on solving real problems
- ✅ Mention verification and safety

**DON'T:**
- ❌ Claim "Nepal's #1" or "biggest" (we're new)
- ❌ Promise instant sales
- ❌ Overuse corporate jargon
- ❌ Hide that we're launching/beta
- ❌ Claim thousands of listings (unless true)
- ❌ Guarantee buyers will purchase
- ❌ Promise financing or insurance

---

## Tone Guidelines

**Voice:** Friendly, honest, helpful  
**Style:** Simple, direct, no-nonsense  
**Perspective:** First-person plural ("we") or second-person ("you")  
**Feeling:** Trustworthy, local, unpretentious

**Examples:**

**Good:**  
- "We're launching in Nepal with free dealer accounts"
- "List your bike, connect with buyers — it's that simple"
- "No commission, no tricks — just a platform that works"

**Bad:**  
- "Revolutionizing Nepal's automotive sector with disruptive technology"
- "Join thousands of satisfied customers" (we don't have thousands yet)
- "Nepal's #1 premium bike marketplace" (too corporate, untrue)
